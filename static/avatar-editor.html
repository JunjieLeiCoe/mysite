How to use:

  1. Upload an image by clicking "Choose Image" or drag & drop
  2. Drag the image to position it within the circle
  3. Use the zoom slider to resize
  4. Use the rotation slider to rotate
  5. Click "Download Avatar" to save your cropped image
  6. Move the downloaded file to static/images/ in your Hugo site
  7. Update hugo.yaml to point to your new avatar
